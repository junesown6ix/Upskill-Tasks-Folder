About Me:

Hello, I am Daniel, an aspiring passionate software developer, hoping to harness a creative flair for crafting engaging and dynamic digital experiences.
My journey into the world of coding started with the curiosity to bring ideas to life, and also, frankly speaking, to be able to land a big role one day,
and hopefully, a remote one, because I hate the outside world.


Started out on this path as a self learner, which proved to be a little challenging more often than not, but those challenges I thrived on,
but at the same time I knew that something was missing. It was the need to belong to a community. That nagging feeling brought me to the door steps of Upskill,
which makes it possible to be a part of a community, connect with people with a shared interest and also a common goal of becoming competent software developers.


The journey so far in this current Upskill cohort has been nothing short of fun, as I have been able to connect with prospective learners like myself,
and also engage with people who are at different stages of their web development careers. We share ideas, discuss problems and aslo potential solutions.
Seeing as we just got started with classes, it is not surprising that things have been fairly basic, which is great, as there are many newbies involved.
But I look forward to more complicating stuff and challenging tasks. By the end of this program I hope that I will come out of it much better than I was when I started.
